ExtraWatch is an AJAX component and module that allows you
to watch your website visitors and bots in real-time from the
administration menu. Specially their IP addresses, countries they come from,
geographical location on a map, which pages they are viewing, their browser
and operating system, it creates daily and all-time stats from these
information plus unique, pageload and total hits statistics. Furthermore,
you can block harmful IP addresses, see blocked attempts stats, evaluate
the trend charts, and create goals based on many parameters. In the frontend,
it can show the top countries information and visit counts for certain
periods of time.

for more information about updates please visit:
http://www.extrawatch.com/
